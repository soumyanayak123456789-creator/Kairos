What is Kairos?

Kairos — Greek for “the decisive moment” — is a proactive AI productivity agent for “The Last-Minute Life Saver.” Instead of passive reminders, it plans your work, prioritizes it, and autonomously books the time on your calendar so deadlines stop sneaking up on you.

1

Perceive

Kairos reads your Google Calendar events and free/busy windows to understand exactly what time you actually have.

2

Plan

Using Gemini, it breaks your goal into subtasks, weighs the deadline against your free time, and decides where focus blocks should go.

3

Act

It books the focus blocks on your calendar within your working hours — and if a deadline looks impossible, it drafts a heads-up message for you to review.

How Kairos saves your deadline

A quick walkthrough with a sample goal — no calendar access needed. Live use on your own calendar needs test-user access (see the sign-in panel).

🎯 “Finish the pitch deck for the investor meeting” — due Friday 6:00 PM

1
Perceive — Kairos reads your calendar: meetings Tue–Wed, evenings free, Thursday afternoon open.
2
Plan — It splits the deck into outline → draft → design → rehearse, and weighs each against the time remaining.
3
Act — It books focus blocks into your free time, each clearly marked as a Kairos block:
✦ Deck — outline & draft · Thu 2:00–4:00 PM
✦ Deck — design & rehearse · Fri 10:00 AM–12:00 PM

If the deadline looked impossible, Kairos would instead draft a heads-up message for you to review — it never sends anything itself.
